Founded in 2007 as Centurion Janitorial Services and recently reinvented as Centurion Facility Services due to the high demand for facility maintenance. CFS has quickly built a reputation as one of the leading providers of commercial cleaning. Our continuous pursuit for perfection has resulted in consistent growth each year.
